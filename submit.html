SUBMIT

We are built on the value of **Independence** and committed to amplifying original, authentic voices—whether you are an emerging or an advanced contributor.

ALIGNMENT & VOICE

We primarily look for two things: alignment with **ARSHI's core values** and a compelling, independent perspective.

CORE FIT

  • **Authenticity:** Unfiltered ideas and honest, personal perspective.
  • **Independence:** Critical thought that avoids institutional or commercial constraint.
  • **Curiosity:** Work that explores cultural complexities and asks difficult questions.

OUR CATEGORIES

  • **Society & Culture:** Identity, social movements, and cultural shifts.
  • **Fashion & Art:** Aesthetics, design critique, and contemporary visual culture.
  • **Music & Film:** Reviews, cultural commentary on sound, cinema, and documentary.

PITCHING YOUR IDEA

Pitch First, Draft Later

We prefer a **simple pitch:** a concise summary (1-2 paragraphs) of your idea, its unique angle, and why it's a vital read *right now*. For emerging writers, please include a short bio; for established writers, links to 1-2 published clips are helpful.

Article Length

We generally publish pieces designed for a **4 to 8 minute read** (approximately 1000–2000 words), but we prioritize impact and depth over strict word count.

Visual Projects

For photography or film proposals, submit a **Treatment** (1-2 pages) outlining the concept and visual references. Please send portfolio links (website, Vimeo, etc.), not large files.

JOIN THE COLLECTIVE

If your perspective aligns with our vision of independence and critique, we welcome your voice.

Email Your Pitch to submissions@arshi.studio

We aim to respond to all pitches within 10 days. Thank you for your interest in ARSHI.
