ABOUT ARSHI

ARSHI is an independent cultural magazine shaped by curiosity.

Back to Home

Born from the need to give **recognition** to emerging voices, ARSHI is a space for writers and artists to publish their work under their own name, and to be read as they are.

We focus on **Society & Culture**, **Fashion & Art**, and **Music & Film**, welcoming contributions that explore intersections and unexpected perspectives. Essays, reportage, interviews, reviews, and visual work all have their place here. Crossovers are encouraged: a piece on politics in fashion, or music in protest culture, belongs in ARSHI.

OUR CORE VALUES

Independence
Free from editorial gatekeeping or institutional agendas.
Recognition
Contributors always publish under their own names.
Authenticity
Original perspectives matter more than polish.
Collaboration
ARSHI grows through collective effort.
Integrity
Facts are checked, sources credited, conflicts of interest disclosed.
Cultural Curiosity
New angles and underrepresented voices are central.
Inclusivity & Diversity
We respect and encourage contributions from LGBTQ+ voices and welcome writers, artists, and creators of all races, genders, origins, and classes.
