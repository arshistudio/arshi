BECOME A COLLABORATOR

Back to Options

ARSHI is an independent magazine built by the people who contribute to it.

Collaborators are the voices that shape ARSHI from within: writers, photographers, editors, and cultural thinkers who contribute regularly and help guide the magazine’s direction. Becoming a collaborator is being part of ARSHI’s DNA: contributing ideas, producing work, and growing with the project. We’re building an independent space that thrives on consistency, curiosity, and cultural depth, and we need people who want to be present and active in that journey.

What it means to collaborate

  • Contribute consistently (monthly, bi-monthly, or quarterly).
  • Take part in theme discussions and brainstorms when possible.
  • Bring your skills and interests to the table, whether through writing, photography, editing, events, or communications.
  • Be part of ARSHI’s Contributors Circle, credited on our website and featured alongside your work.

What you gain

  • A platform to publish your work under your own name.
  • A space to experiment, question, and create without editorial gatekeeping.
  • Visibility as part of a growing independent magazine.
  • A place in a collective that values independence, recognition, and authenticity.

⚠️ Please note:

ARSHI is an independent, self-funded project. At this stage, contributions are unpaid. Collaborators retain ownership of their work and are always credited by name. As the magazine grows, we hope to build towards sustainability and fair compensation for contributors.
