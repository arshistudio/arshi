Submissions & Pitches

ARSHI is always looking for original, independent, and critical voices across all our categories. We prioritize **bold perspectives** and work that challenges conventional narratives.

What We Are Looking For

We accept pitches across the following primary categories and formats:

  • **Society & Culture:** Long-form essays, critical theory, and investigative reportage on social and political movements.
  • **Fashion & Art:** Deep dives into visual culture, textile design, analog photography, and gallery critique.
  • **Music & Film:** Reviews, artist interviews, and historical analysis of independent cinema and underground music scenes.

Pitch Guidelines

Your submission email should include these three elements:

  1. **A working title** (Use the bold, condensed style as inspiration).
  2. **A concise, 3-paragraph summary** of your piece: What is the thesis, why is it relevant now, and what sources will you use?
  3. **Your bio** and links to 1–2 relevant published works.

We target pieces between **1,200 and 2,000 words** (6–10 minutes reading time).
