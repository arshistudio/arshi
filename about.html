About

Arshi.studio is an independent publishing platform dedicated to giving space to freelance journalists, writers, and cultural voices who choose not to affiliate with a single outlet.

We believe that journalism and storytelling should be free from rigid structures and institutional branding. Here, every article is published under the writer’s own name — no hidden agendas, no editorial gatekeeping, no need to fit into a magazine’s house style.

Our focus is on society & culture, fashion & art, music & film — subjects that shape how we live, think, and express ourselves. By bringing together diverse voices from different backgrounds, we aim to create a living archive of perspectives that might otherwise remain unseen.

Arshi.studio is not a newsroom, not a blog, not a corporate platform. It’s a collective space where independence comes first and where readers can discover authentic work directly from the writers who create it.
