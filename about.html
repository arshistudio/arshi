ABOUT ARSHI

ARSHI is an independent cultural magazine shaped by curiosity.

Back to Home

Born from the need to give recognition to emerging voices, ARSHI is a space for writers and artists to publish their work under their own name, and to be read as they are.

We focus on Society & Culture, Fashion & Art, and Music & Film, welcoming contributions that explore intersections and unexpected perspectives. Essays, reportage, interviews, reviews, and visual work all have their place here. Crossovers are encouraged: a piece on politics in fashion, or music in protest culture, belongs in ARSHI.

OUR CORE VALUES

01

Editorial Autonomy

We are proudly independent, ensuring our content is free from institutional agendas and external gatekeeping.

02

Authorial Credit

Every contributor is published under their own name, guaranteeing full recognition and perpetual ownership of their work.

03

Genuine Perspective

We prioritize original voices and honest exploration over overly polished or commercialized narratives.

04

Collective Growth

ARSHI thrives as a communal project, fostered by the shared expertise and collective effort of our collaborators.

05

Editorial Rigor

We uphold the highest standards of integrity, committing to thorough fact-checking, proper source attribution, and full transparency.

06

Cultural Curiosity

Our mission is to encourage constant exploration, seeking out new angles and amplifying voices that are often underrepresented.

07

Open Platform

We are fundamentally committed to providing an inclusive space that respects and encourages contributions from all backgrounds, regardless of race, gender, identity, or origin.
