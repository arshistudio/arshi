ABOUT ARSHI

ARSHI is an **independent cultural magazine** shaped by curiosity, providing a space for writers and artists to publish their work under their own name.

OUR MISSION

Born from the need to give recognition to **emerging voices**, ARSHI is a space where contributors retain their rights and are read as they are. We seek out new angles and amplify voices that are often underrepresented.

We focus on **Society & Culture**, **Fashion & Art**, and **Music & Film**, welcoming contributions that explore intersections and unexpected perspectives. Crossovers are encouraged: a piece on politics in fashion, or music in protest culture, belongs in ARSHI.

CORE VALUES

01

EDITORIAL AUTONOMY

We are independent, ensuring our content is free from institutional agendas and gatekeeping.

02

AUTHORIAL CREDIT

Every contributor is published under their own name, guaranteeing full recognition.

03

GENUINE PERSPECTIVE

We prioritize original voices and honest exploration over overly polished or commercialized narratives.

04

COLLECTIVE GROWTH

ARSHI thrives as a communal project, fostered by the shared expertise and collective effort of our collaborators.
